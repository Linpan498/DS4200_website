About Me

Hi, I'm Lin, a third-year student at Northeastern University's Khoury College of Computer Sciences. I am pursuing a combined degree in Data Science and Mathematics. I am passionate about turning raw data into meaningful insights and building tools that help people make better decisions. My academic journey has given me a strong foundation in machine learning, data visualization, statistical modeling, and number theory. I love exploring the intersection of math and technology.

Through Northeastern's cooperative education program, I have gained valuable hands-on experience in the industry. I completed a six-month co-op at TJX Companies, where I worked on the Risk Assurance team building data pipelines with Snowflake, creating interactive dashboards in Power BI, and developing a machine learning model for data deduplication. These experiences taught me how to translate complex analytical problems into practical solutions in a professional setting.

Beyond academics and work, I am actively involved in the campus community. I serve as the Director of Public Relations for Northeastern's Chinese Students and Scholars Association, where I coordinate outreach efforts and help build connections among students. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new cities, trying different cuisines, and continuously learning new tools and technologies.
