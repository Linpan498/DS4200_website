My Experience

Co-op at TJX Companies

During my six-month co-op at TJX Companies from July to December 2025, I worked as a Data Analyst on the Risk Assurance team. My primary responsibilities included designing and maintaining data pipelines using Snowflake SQL, building interactive dashboards in Power BI to track key risk metrics, and developing a machine learning deduplication system that improved data quality across multiple internal databases. I collaborated closely with cross-functional teams including IT, finance, and compliance to ensure that our data solutions met the needs of various stakeholders.

Before TJX, I also completed an internship at China Great Wall Securities in the corporate finance division. This role introduced me to financial analysis and capital markets. I assisted senior analysts with due diligence research, prepared financial models, and contributed to client-facing presentations. Working in a fast-paced financial environment taught me how to manage multiple deadlines and communicate analytical findings effectively. This experience solidified my interest in combining data science with financial applications and business intelligence.
